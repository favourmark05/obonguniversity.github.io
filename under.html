Undergraduate Programme

Obong University provides quality undergraduate Programmes in Natural & Applied Sciences, Management Sciences, Social Sciences and Arts & Humanities in an environment of academic excellence. The University also offers opportunities for spiritual growth and encouragement for entrepreneurial skills development.
