SCHOOL OF BASIC STUDIES

Obong University's School of Basic Studies was established to enable young school leavers
who couldn't make it in Jamb Exam to have a chance of gaining admission into the univeristy.

Requirements for Admission into the Programme:

Candidates must possess 5 credits in SSCE/NECO/NABTEB in relevant subjects. At the end of the programme, students will write the
Joint Universities Preliminary Examination Board (JUPEB) Examination, which will qualify them for Direct Entry (D/E) admission through
JAMB into year two (2) in any FAculty/Department of their choice in Obong University or any other University of their choice.



The following are the Fields of Study Covered:

Arts and Humanities
Natural and Applied Scieces
Management Sciences
Social Sciences
Engineering
Basic Medicals
Medicine and Surgery
Engineering
Agricultural Sciences

Entry Requirements:Candidates must possess 5 O/Level Credits in WAEC,NECO and NABTEB
